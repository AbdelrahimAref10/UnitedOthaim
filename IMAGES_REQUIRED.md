## Image Requirements

### Hero Slider Images (hero-slide-1.jpg, hero-slide-2.jpg, hero-slide-3.jpg)
- **Size**: 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Format**: JPG
- **Content**: Professional business images showcasing:
  - Company buildings/facilities
  - Team collaboration
  - Modern office spaces
  - Industrial facilities
- **Style**: High quality, professional photography

### About Section Image (about-section.jpg)
- **Size**: 1200x800px
- **Format**: JPG
- **Content**: Company headquarters or main facility
- **Style**: Professional, welcoming

### Service Images (services/*.jpg)
- **Size**: 800x600px
- **Format**: JPG
- **Content**: Each image should represent the specific service:
  - `elevator.jpg`: Elevators in buildings
  - `contracting.jpg`: Construction sites
  - `manufacturing.jpg`: Manufacturing facilities
  - `development.jpg`: Real estate development
  - `investments.jpg`: Business/financial imagery
  - `hotel.jpg`: Hotel services
  - `consulting.jpg`: Business consulting
  - `lowCurrent.jpg`: Electrical/low current systems
  - `happyCheese.jpg`: Food/cheese products

### Team Images (team/*.jpg)
- **Size**: 600x600px (Square)
- **Format**: JPG
- **Content**: Professional headshots of team members
- **Style**: Professional portraits, consistent lighting and background

### Client Logos (clients/*.png)
- **Size**: 300x150px (or proportional)
- **Format**: PNG (with transparency preferred)
- **Content**: Client company logos
- **Style**: Clean, professional logos

## Notes
- All images should be optimized for web (compressed but high quality)
- Use consistent color schemes that match the teal theme
- Ensure images are properly licensed for commercial use
- Consider dark mode compatibility for images with text overlays
